Cork Road College Campus

Located inside WIT's Main Campus on Cork Road, Waterford. This is often recommended for first years as it feels more safer as it located inside the Main Campus at WIT. Therefore students of this course would only need to walk to their lecture's building outside their doors. Students will have their own single room, while sharing the apartments's kitchen and sitting room. It has great security with CCTV system and a live in warden. It also offers services such as a laundry facility.

Spaces are very limited, if you want a room you need to deposit €300. Students can pay for a room per semester, from Sept - Dec and Jan - May. Don't worry, you can cancel it but make sure its not too late. The total payment for semester one is around €2,500 while semester 2 is €3,000 (Single Room). Students can pay this in monthly installiments if they wish. We recommend checking out the offical WIT website in relation for applying for a room as they have the officals forms to apply. City Campus

Located near the heart of Waterford City. It is also a 15th minute walk from the main campus on Cork Road. This place is ideal for anyone doing a course in the College Street Campus and for 2nd, 3rd and 4th year students. This is a much bigger complex than the other accommodation. City Campus has simular services like the one on Cork Road but has advantages. Such as being a 10 minute walk into the city's shops and deluxe ensuite bedrooms. Like the cork road campus, payment needs a deposit of €300 for booking a room. A single room on city campus for semester 1 is around €2,400 and semester 2 is €2,900. For more information, we recommend the offical WIT website, where you can apply for a room and find extra information.

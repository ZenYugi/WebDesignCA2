Wit Arena

Home for WIT students who are proactive in their sports. However, non students can use this facility too. Opening in September 2016, this venue has held sport events by the college. As well as being the training grounds for the college's sports teams (Soccer / GAA). Students can use its fantastic "Edge Gym" to work on their fitness goals or break a sweat after a day of college work. Memberships for all WIT students is €150 for a year, a semester is €90. With a membership, you have unlimited access to the gym, FREE parking, access to 2km running trail and many more. Click the link below to find out more!

Shopping

Waterford City has the City Square Shopping Centre. A popular place for the people of Waterford for their shopping needs, such as groceries or the lastest brand clothes. Shops such as JD, Dunnes Stores, Bookstation, and Gamestop are all located inside this shopping centre. Very popular among college students as some of the shops like JD offer discounts for students. If you feeling hungry, City Square offers O'Brien's Sandwiches and the Bagel Factory. Click the link below to find more information reguarding offers and parking.

City Square Website

Tramore Seaside Town

Located outside of Waterford City in a fourteen minute drive. If you are student, you can get a discount on the bus as the return ticket is only €2.00, a very good deal. Tramore offers students various of activities. Such as the tramore races, amusement park, the japnese gardens and a beautiful beach. There is also lots of places to eat and drink, the popular ones for students are the Pier Cafe and Dooly's Chipper. Around Dooly's Chipper corner is the Atlantic Arcade & Casino, by the way they offer FREE tea and coffee there!!

Tramore Website

Accommodation

This can be the most difficult aspect starting first year, getting a roof over your head. Especially if you live far from home or overseas. The great news is WIT offers two accommodation facilities in Waterford. The appartments on the Cork Road Main campus and the City Campus located near the city's center. If you can't secure accommodation, there are other options such as sharing a house with other students or living under a host's family.

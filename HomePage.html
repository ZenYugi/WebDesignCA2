Bachelor of Business in Recreation & Sports Mangement

WIT or soon to be called SETU (South East Technology Unniversity) is home to various undergraduate courses offered in the South East of Ireland. This course, the Bachelor of Business in Recreation and Sports Management offers a degree where the student will work in both the sport and business industry. Our website Plareport, will showcase why we recommend joining this course this coming Auguest. You will find information about the course itself and Waterford City. Don't be afraid to get in touch on our contact us section or our contact us page.

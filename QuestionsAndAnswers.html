Questions and Answers

Q1: When do students get their holidays
A1: This various each year of the course, however at WIT. There is usually a two to three week break in December for the Xmas holidays. Exams are finshed for that semester by then too. There is also the Easter Break in April which lasts for two weeks.
Q2: Do WIT have any clubs that students can joined?
A2: Yes, WIT wants first-year students to join its various clubs and societies. A great way of making new friends at WIT. Keep an eye out for the club/societies open day where student gain more information and apply for the clubs they want to join
Q3: What kind of transport could I use to get into Waterford City?
A3: There are various of ways of arrivaing in Waterford City. Waterford City have multiple of bus services coming in/out of places such as Dublin and Cork as well as the trains too.

Q5: How long does a typical day of college lasts for a student?
A5: This depends on each course, usually the first class starts at 9:00AM and can last a hour, sometimes two hours depending on timetable. The last lectures stop at 5:00pm, again depends on a course's timetable.

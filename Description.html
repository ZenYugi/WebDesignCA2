What is it?

This is a three year fulltime undergraduate course. Students are taught various skills that they can use within the sport and business industries. What is Recreation and Sports Management? Recreation is people playing sports but is less competitive and is seen more as a relaxing activity but still fun for everybody involve. Making it less stressful physically and mentally. This course allows students to learn how to oragnise leisure events and be in a role of a couch. For example, organising a sports day for children in the local parish. However, there is also subjects that teaches students how to be a personal trainer inside the gym, which can be more competitive if the person they are working with are proactive in sports such as boxing or soccer.

As for the businness side of the course, it is more related to the sports industry. Which can make the business more engaging for the students. Students are taught subjects such as accounting, hr and economics. This opens the door for work in either the sport and businness industries, or even both. In this course, students will work on these subjects in two buildings in Waterford City. For lectures, where students sit inside and take notes while the lecturer or teacher is teaching the class. This will be done in the Tourism & Leisure Building on WIT Main Campus, Cork Road, Waterford City, Co. Waterford. For practicals, where the students will be working on their skills taught from the lectures. This will be done in the WIT Arena on Carriganore, Co. Waterford

What is unique about it?

Since this course is a ordinary bachelor degree, students can pursue into the honours course, Bachelor of Business (Hons) in Recreation and Sport Management. Students can get work placement in year 3, semester 3. This is where they can go out and work in job to gain real work experince. The duration of this is 14 weeks, starting from January in year 3. Students can work in an area they personally see theirselves enjoying and wish to do in the future. Work abroad is also an option too.

There are subjects in this course that gives students real world qualifications. Such as being a persoanl trainer. Fitness in gyms is a growing market, they even may gain a work placement at the WIT Arena or the other gyms in Waterford such as Goldstone and Xtreme. There is also other qualifications in first aid and swim teaching. In year 3 semester 5 (Auguest - December), students are offered a choice of two subjects. By this point, they can choose if they want to go down the business path or sports path.

About

Why Us?

We as a small company wanted to help WIT with increasing the number of undergraducate students. We highlighted this course because we believe Waterford City is a great place for students interested in a sports degree. Our own staff members go to the WIT Arena sport facility outside of work hours. They go there for soccer and hurling training but also working a sweat in the gym too. Most of our team are former WIT Students too, so we all felt motivated in carrying out making this website for any leaving cert students, parents or mature students. We been a company for over five years now, we hope you find this website useful and if you need any questions, don't forget to contact us. Have a great day!

More Info

Our company MunsterTech, was setup in 2015 and is located in Waterford City. It was founded by John Power and Amy Rose. Both were young web developers who gained experience by making websites for clients in Dublin, Cork and around the world. They are also former WIT Students and they have hired WIT students doing their work placements at the company. So if you know anyone who is doing a work placement, you can contact us via email!

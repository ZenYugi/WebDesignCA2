Course Details

Introduction

This is a broad course as it mixes subjects relating to sports and business. The course duration is three years. Not only will the student gain a bachelor degree of this course. They could gain qualfications for peronal training and swimming. Students will attend the class on the main campus and doing practical lessons at the WIT Arena. Don't worry, these locations are not too far from each other. There is a shuttle bus that takes students to these places. Best thing about this for students is that it is FREE to use, but you need to show your student card. To find more information about the course, such as what are the subjects are and the jobs you can enter in, click on buttons beside this section.
